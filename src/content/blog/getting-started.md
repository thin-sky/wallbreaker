# Getting Started: How to Shop

Shopping with us is easy! Here's everything you need to know.

## Browsing Products

Explore our product collections to find exactly what you're looking for:

- **Browse by Category**: Find products organized by type
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific items
- **Filter & Sort**: Narrow down results by price, size, or availability

## Adding to Cart

When you find something you love:

1. Select your size/variant
2. Choose quantity
3. Click "Add to Cart"
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

## Checkout Process

Our secure checkout is powered by Fourthwall:

1. **Review Cart**: Verify your items and quantities
2. **Shipping Info**: Enter your delivery address
3. **Payment**: Choose your payment method
4. **Confirm**: Review and complete your order

## Order Tracking

After your order is placed:

- You'll receive a confirmation email
- Track your shipment with the provided tracking number
- Contact us if you have any questions

Check out our [FAQ page](/faq) or reach out to our support team.
